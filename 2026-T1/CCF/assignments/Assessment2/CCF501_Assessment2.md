## Assessment Task
Submit a report that analyses and determines the application of various cloud services and deployment models.

Please refer to the **Instructions** for details on how to complete this task.

## Context
Cloud solutions vary by sector:

- **Automotive** — companies like F1 (Formula One car racing) use cloud solutions to run aerodynamic simulations to make a next-generation car that is 70% faster than previous models.
- **Multimedia** — companies like Snapchat decreased the latency speed of sending *Snaps* by 20%.
- **Pharmaceutical** — companies like Moderna utilise cloud solutions to sequence its mRNA COVID-19 vaccine in just 48 hours.

In Weeks 5 to 8, we explored major public cloud providers, the comparison among them, advanced cloud concepts, models and some deployment case studies. By analysing real-life case studies, you will:

- Understand how cloud fits in modern computing requirements
- Understand and categorise the different cloud service models that can be utilised to solve a challenge
- Identify key cloud service offerings for recommendation to major industry providers
- Compare, contrast and validate different deployment models utilising services by major cloud providers

## Instructions

### Report
Prepare a 2,900-word report (+/-10%), excluding references, based on the three case studies provided by your Learning Facilitator.

**Case study selection criteria (per brief):**
- Each of the three case studies are from different service and deployment models
- They cater for different service sectors of cloud-like web applications, big data, IoT, artificial intelligence and so on
- Each uses a variety of services from providers
- The case studies are taken from popular public cloud providers

### Report Structure

1. **Introduction** (~400 words) — state basic information relevant to the report and introduce relevant points relating to the case studies (e.g. background on cloud computing). Written in complete sentences and paragraphs.

2. **Main Body** — three sections of ~750 words each, one per case study. For each case study:
   - Analyse the case study
   - Analyse the service model for the case study, including the different services provided by the cloud provider
   - Compare and contrast the different deployment models for the case study
   - Analyse the application of the different services used in the case study
   - Write a reflection on the case study

3. **Conclusion** (~250 words) — summary of analysis, links to the purpose of the assessment, areas identified for further investigation. No tables, graphs, diagrams, or dot points. Written in complete sentences and paragraphs.

4. **References** — minimum 10 to 12 APA-style references.
