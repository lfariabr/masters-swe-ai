# Advance Communication Techniques

## About this Course

Communication skills. It’s an integral part of your job.  You need it to collaborate with your colleagues, to explain complex ideas to your clients, and to persuade someone to see your point of view.  And yet it is considered a “soft skill” that is often secondary to the technical skills that you bring to your role. It’s also common to stereotype people who work in IT or tech as introverted, brain-based individuals who are not naturally adept at speaking in front of people or communicating in general. 

And what do we mean by ‘advanced’ communication skills in this short course?   We will be going beyond what is considered the basic skills of communication so you can better persuade, influence and lead people to accept changes or make a decision.  Some may consider advanced communication skills as essential for someone in a leadership position, as these are skills that help guide and direct communication between you and the recipient/s of your message to reach specific goals and outcomes.  

## Course Objectives

Recognise what makes a confident communicator, particularly in the IT industry
Consider a range of practical tools on understanding the audience, structuring your message, and delivering with impact.
Develop an understanding of the application of these skills and tools in challenging communication situations.
